Hello! This is the north dallas astrodynamics research team's research on the efficiency of different muon detection methods (scintillation, geiger counters LiPo etc) on the ground and in the air in a high altitude weather balloon. We started this project since there was a scarce amount of information on muon detectors when I wanted to learn about them. In all, this project aims to add more clarity in the world of muon detectors, scintillators, best material types, and the strengths and weaknesses of the detectors we test. The cosmic watch system is the ultimate low cost muon detection system, and it will be compared in a multitude of different configurations in order to prove the best method of muon detection and best material for scinilation for researchers at universities worldwide. We plan to publish a paper detailing our findings after the conclusion of our tests.
